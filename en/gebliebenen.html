Die Gebliebenen

Albin Wildner / Austria / 2023 / 30' / short, documentary
Languages: German, Slovenian

The last remaining masters of their craft from Austria, Hungary and Slovenia come together in the last garment factory in Austria. The film tells the story of the a dying profession in Europe at a time of fast fashion.
A discussion with the film-maker will follow the film.
