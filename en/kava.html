Chirimbar coffee

Gorazd Orešnik / Slovenia / 2017 / 12' / KO BOGA TE MOLIM / short / drama / comedy
Language: Slovenian

What happens when a tired husband asks his wife to make him coffee after a hard day? The answer lies in the film Čirimbarska kava, which includes elements of drama, horror, romantic drama and comedy. The film was created under the direction of deaf and mute creators.
