Martin pečar trio

CONCERT

Music from the 1930s that goes easily to the ear, the so-called standards for jazz performers. Stylistically, they range from swing, bossa-nova, tango to classical influences, as played by guitar virtuoso Django Reinhardt himself. The freedom that such music allows through improvised themes and articulation, however, draws out the individual performer and places them at the forefront of the moment. The energetic melos unleashes the zeitgeist of the genre's origins, creating a magical ambiance. The performers in this trio are guitarists Martin Pečar and Jernej Štravs and double bassist Patrick Gyurkač.
