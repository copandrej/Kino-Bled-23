Sklop kratkih naravoljubnih filmov o Vodi



1. On, In and Under the Sava


Rožle Bregar, Rok Rozman / Slovenia / 2023 / 5' / Slovenian / short nature films

Four friends, armed with a raft, snorkels and fins, sail down the Sava River with one mission: to find the elusive river monsters that live in the depths in order to help protect the river from dams.

2. The Last of the Ferrymen


Toni Kristian Tikkanen, Ville Asikainen / Finland / 2023 / 15' / Finnish / short documentary

The film tells the story of Finnish wader Taisto Kainulainen and the great river Vuoksi.

3. Expedition on 3 Rivers


Irfan Alijagić, Ervin Duratović / BIH / 2022 / 51' / Bosnian / short adventure documentary

Growing up on the river Una, they dreamt of doing what no one before them had managed to do. After years of preparation, the time has come to turn their dream into reality. A story of courage and adventure through the unexplored areas of the Una River, tributaries of the Krka River and the Unac River.
