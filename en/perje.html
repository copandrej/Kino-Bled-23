The Feathers That Smell of The Sea

Žoel Kastelic / SLO / 2019 / 3' / animated
Language: Slovenian

The story of the cormorant, the symbol of being split between two worlds. Just as people can live torn between two worlds, so can the cormorant, who is a bird living the life of a fish. Such life is not impossible, but the cormorant still has to surface to get some air and dry its wings.
