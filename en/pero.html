Pero

Damjan Kozole / Slovenia / 2023 / 99' / feature film / biographical
Language: Slovenian

"For me it was always Pero, not Peter. In 2002, we filmed the movie Rezervni deli, and that was the beginning of our friendship and collaboration. Then we made another 6 films together. At the end of 2019, we started preparing a new film. About him and his demons. In March 2020, at the beginning of the corona crisis, Pero committed suicide. We didn't even take a shot. First of all, I decided that this movie would not happen. But I felt the need to record it anyway. To say goodbye to Pero with him.” - Damjan Kozole
