The Swimmer

Frank Perry / USA / 1968 / 95' / Feature Film / Surrealist Drama
Language: English

Wealthy advertiser Ned Merrill is visiting a friend when he notices the abundance of backyard swimming pools that populate their upscale suburb. Ned suddenly decides that he would like to travel the eight miles back to his own home by simply swimming through all the pools in town. Soon Ned's journey becomes harrowing; at each house, in one way or another, he faces a reminder of his romantic, domestic, and economic failures.
