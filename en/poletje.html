MGC Bled

Summer of '91

Žiga Virc / Slovenia / 2021 / 72' / documentary
Language: Slovenian

Žiga Virc's (Houston, We Have a Problem!) archival documentary provides a new and unencumbered insight into the everyday life of the inhabitants of Slovenia at the crossroads of history. Created through a large-scale campaign in which people were invited to send in their home movies, the film puts the viewer in an audiovisual time machine and takes them back to the chaotic period before, during and after Slovenia's independence.
The film will be followed by a discussion with the filmmaker.

Free entry!
