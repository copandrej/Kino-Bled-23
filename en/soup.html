Wednesday, 21. 6. 2023 at 19.00
Zdraviliški park, Bled

Pay for Soup

Concert

Ljubljana quartet Pay for Soup consists of bassist and guitarist André Droga, guitarist Dan Caf, drummer Luka Hren and keyboardist Martin Vasle. They told Radio Študent that they see their music as a reflection of the situations and moods that the same music paints. It sounds more nocturnal than daytime, more cool than honest, and more immersive than narrative.

Free entry!
