MGC Bled

Speaking of Pastors

Jan Mühlethaler / Switzerland / 2022 / 7’ 12’’ / short
Langugae: Swiss German

In the afternoon in the Tea-Room, two acquaintances meet by chance after a funeral. How do the two relate to each other? Is it only the coffee and the cake that connects them, or is it more? Soon we find ourselves in the midst of a tangle of what they have experienced and heard; What is the story of aunt Trudi, who was killed by a meteorite while picking currants? The Pope's visit at Visp airport, a cat named Rambo or a never-ending movie show at the Astoria cinema? Who calls himself butt and who is this Clemens?
