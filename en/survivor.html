Talking to a survivor

Milena Olip / Austria / 2022 / 8' 17'' / feature
Languages: English, Slovenian

Every day, a man leaves his picturesque mountain farm to go to a neighbouring town, where he works in shifts on a conveyor belt to help produce products. These - both in his immediate neighbourhood and on the other side of the world - can cause extreme hardship.
