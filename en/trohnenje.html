Rotting 2022

Tomo Križnar and Bojana Pivk Križnar / Slovenia / 2022 / 56' / documentary film
Language: Slovenian

A shocking documentary that is not only a film about the rotting flesh of lepers in the Nuba Mountains and the Blue Nile, but also a film about the rotting of ethics, hearts and minds in privileged parts of the world. The film will be followed by a conversation with activist, humanitarian and director Tomo Križnar.
