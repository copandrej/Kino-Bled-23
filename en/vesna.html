VESNA

František Čap / Yugoslavia (Slovenia) / 1953 / 95' / full-length feature film / black and white
Language: Slovenian

The first real comedy created in Slovenia tells about the life of high school students. Three classmates who have to take their matriculation exams agree that Samo, one of them, must win the daughter of Professor Cosinus and thus provide the math assignments. When he gets to know Samo Vesna better, he really falls in love with her and doesn't want to hear anything about the tasks anymore. Meanwhile, Vesna learns about their plan from Sam's classmate and refuses to see Sam again. But in the end, things clear up and the main characters become friends again (and more).
