Zanox – Tveganja in stranski učinki

Benő Baranyi / Hungary / 2022 / 88’ / feature film / science fiction, comedy, coming of age
Language: Hungarian

Misi, a shy 18-year-old, has been in love with his classmate Janko for years, but he doesn't dare approach her. For panic attacks, he takes the experimental drug Zanox, manufactured by a local pharmaceutical company, which has unexpected side effects. At Misa, on the eve of graduation, he discovers that if he drinks Hungarian homemade apple pálinka (brandy) after consuming the medicine, he can travel to the past. This allows him to perform very well in the final exam in history and to get closer to Janko. However, prom night is overshadowed by a crime: one of the girls at the prom is attacked in the woods next to the pub. In order to gain Janko's favor, Misi uses pálinka magic to trick the attacker and prevent the crime, but she bites off more than she can chew and ends up in seemingly unsolvable situations.
