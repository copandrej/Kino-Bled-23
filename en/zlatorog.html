Legend of the Golden Horn

Lea Vučko / Slovenia / 2022 / 14' / without dialogues / short animated film
Language: Slovenian

On a winter evening, a young hunter goes to the mountains. He wants to get rid of the girl who left him for a wealthy stranger. Visions of the girl haunt him and drive him to despair. That's when his dark side comes to life, telling him: "Kill the holy Golden Horn and win back the beloved girl!"
